Global Journeys: Most Beautiful World Destinations

City of Gold

Explore Dubai

  • Downtown Dubai

  • Home to the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and dancing fountains. The center of modern luxury and architectural marvels.

  • Best: Nov-Mar City Center
  • Burj Khalifa Shopping
  • Palm Jumeirah

  • The world-famous man-made island shaped like a palm tree. Features luxury resorts like Atlantis and pristine beaches.

  • Best: All Year Palm Island
  • Atlantis Beaches
  • Desert Safari

  • Experience the golden dunes with thrilling dune bashing, camel rides, and traditional Bedouin camp dinners under the stars.

  • Best: Oct-Apr Outer Dubai
  • Adventure Culture
  • Abu Dhabi: Capital Luxury

  • A short drive from Dubai, Abu Dhabi offers the magnificent Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, Ferrari World, and the Louvre Abu Dhabi.

  • Best: Nov-Mar Capital City
  • Grand Mosque Culture
  • Dubai Marina

  • An affluent residential neighborhood known for The Beach at JBR, upscale dining, and relaxing boat cruises along the skyscraper-lined canal.

  • Best: All Year Waterfront
  • Yacht Club Nightlife
  • Dubai Miracle Garden

  • The world's largest natural flower garden with over 150 million flowers arranged in colorful arches and patterns.

  • Best: Nov-May Flower Garden
  • Flowers Views
Land of Smiles

Explore Thailand

  • Bangkok

  • A vibrant capital known for ornate shrines, floating markets, and energetic street life. A blend of tradition and modernity.

  • Best: Nov-Mar Capital City
  • Temples Nightlife
  • Phuket

  • Thailand's largest island, famous for its clear waters, limestone cliffs, and luxury seaside resorts.

  • Best: Nov-Apr Island
  • Beaches Island Hopping
  • Pattaya

  • A coastal city on the Gulf of Thailand, known for its beaches, cabaret shows, and 24-hour excitement.

  • Best: Nov-Apr Coastal City
  • Beaches Entertainment
  • Krabi: Nature Paradise

  • Famous for its emerald green waters, limestone cliffs, and Railay Beach. A heaven for rock climbers and island hoppers.

  • Best: Nov-Apr Coastal Region
  • Cliffs Railay Beach
  • Chiang Mai

  • Explore the mountainous northern city with its ornate temples, night markets, and ethical elephant sanctuaries.

  • Best: Nov-Feb North Thailand
  • Temples Mountains
  • Koh Samui

  • Thailand's second largest island, known for its palm-fringed beaches, coconut groves and dense, mountainous rainforest.

  • Best: Dec-Sep Island
  • Beaches Luxury
Island of Gods

Explore Bali, Indonesia

  • Ubud

  • The cultural heart of Bali. Surrounded by rainforests and terraced rice paddies, dotted with Hindu temples and shrines.

  • Best: Apr-Oct Central Bali
  • Culture Nature
  • Kuta & Seminyak

  • Famous for surfing, stunning sunsets, and high-end dining. The center of action and beach life in Bali.

  • Best: Apr-Oct South Bali
  • Surf Nightlife
  • Nusa Penida

  • A rugged island slightly off Bali, known for Kelingking Beach, dinosaur-shaped cliffs, and crystal clear diving spots.

  • Best: May-Sep Offshore Island
  • Adventure Views
  • Uluwatu: Cliffside Beauty

  • Famous for its cliff-top temple, world-class surfing breaks, and stunning Kecak fire dances at sunset.

  • Best: Apr-Oct South Bali
  • Temple Surfing
  • Tanah Lot

  • An iconic rock formation home to the ancient Hindu pilgrimage temple Pura Tanah Lot, offering the most beautiful sunsets in Bali.

  • Best: All Year Coastal Temple
  • Sunset Iconic
  • Mount Batur

  • An active volcano popular for sunrise treks. Offers spectacular views of the crater lake and surrounding highlands.

  • Best: May-Sep Volcano Trek
  • Hiking Sunrise
