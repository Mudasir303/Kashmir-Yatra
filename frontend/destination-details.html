Srinagar: The Venice of East

Explore Srinagar: The Crown Jewel of Kashmir

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, is a breathtaking city nestled in the Kashmir Valley. Known as the "Venice of the East," Srinagar is famous for its pristine Dal Lake, enchanting Mughal gardens, and traditional houseboats that offer a unique stay experience. The city perfectly blends natural beauty with rich cultural heritage, making it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking tranquility and authentic Kashmiri hospitality.

Experience the magic of gliding across Dal Lake on a traditional shikara at sunrise, surrounded by the majestic Himalayan peaks. Visit the magnificent Mughal gardens like Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi, which showcase the grandeur of Mughal architecture and landscaping. Explore the old city's bustling markets, where you can find exquisite Pashmina shawls, hand-knotted carpets, and traditional Kashmiri handicrafts. Don't miss the spiritual experience at Shankaracharya Temple, perched atop a hill offering panoramic views of the entire valley.
