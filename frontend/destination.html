Explore Domestic & International Destinations

Paradise on Earth

Domestic: Kashmir

  • Srinagar: The Venice of East

  • Experience the magic of Dal Lake with shikara rides, luxury houseboats, and stunning Mughal gardens. The summer capital of Kashmir offers authentic Kashmiri hospitality.

  • Best: Mar-Oct Altitude: 1,585m
  • Dal Lake Mughal Gardens Houseboats
  • Gulmarg: Winter Sports Capital

  • Asia's highest cable car takes you to snow-capped peaks. World-class skiing, snowboarding, and breathtaking Himalayan views await adventure enthusiasts.

  • Best: Dec-Mar (Snow) 51km from Srinagar
  • Gondola Ride Skiing Golf Course
  • Pahalgam: Valley of Shepherds

  • Scenic meadows along Lidder River, base camp for Amarnath Yatra. Explore Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and enjoy horse riding through pine forests.

  • Best: Apr-Oct 95km from Srinagar
  • Betaab Valley River Rafting Trekking
  • Sonamarg: Meadow of Gold

  • Gateway to Ladakh with pristine Thajiwas Glacier. Golden meadows, alpine lakes, and snow-covered peaks create a photographer's paradise.

  • Best: May-Sep 80km from Srinagar
  • Thajiwas Glacier Camping Photography
  • Yusmarg: The Hidden Gem

  • Offbeat paradise away from crowds. Untouched meadows surrounded by pine forests offer tranquility and stunning mountain views for nature lovers.

  • Best: May-Sep 47km from Srinagar
  • Meadows Nature Walks Offbeat
  • Doodhpathri: Valley of Milk

  • Named for milky white streams flowing through lush green meadows. A pristine day-trip destination perfect for picnics and relaxation.

  • Best: Jun-Sep 42km from Srinagar
  • Meadows Day Trip Picnic Spot
Land of Gods

Domestic: Himachal Pradesh

  • Manali: Valley of Gods

  • A high-altitude resort town popular for backpacking, trekking in Parvati Valley, and skiing in Solang Valley.

  • Best: Oct-Jun Altitude: 2,050m
  • Solang Valley Rohtang
  • Shimla: Queen of Hills

  • The capital of Himachal Pradesh, known for its colonial architecture, The Ridge, and the scenic Kalka-Shimla toy train.

  • Best: Mar-Jun Altitude: 2,276m
  • The Ridge Mall Road
  • Kasol: Mini Israel

  • A hamlet in the Kullu district situated in Parvati Valley, famous for its Israeli inhabitants, trekking trails, and hippie culture.

  • Best: Oct-Jun Altitude: 1,580m
  • Parvati River Trekking
Emerald Isles

Domestic: Andaman & Nicobar

  • Havelock Island

  • Famous for Radhanagar Beach, one of the best beaches in Asia. A paradise for scuba diving and snorkeling enthusiasts.

  • Best: Oct-May Ferry from Port Blair
  • Radhanagar Beach Scuba
  • Port Blair: Capital City

  • The definition of history and beauty. Visit the infamous Cellular Jail, Ross Island, and enjoy water sports at Corbyn's Cove.

  • Best: Oct-May Capital City
  • Cellular Jail History
  • Neil Island

  • Known for its biodiversity, unexplored coral reefs, white sandy beaches, and tropical woodlands. A peaceful escape.

  • Best: Oct-May 37km from Port Blair
  • Coral Reefs Serenity
City of Gold

International: Dubai

  • Downtown Dubai

  • Home to the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and dancing fountains. The center of modern luxury and architectural marvels.

  • Best: Nov-Mar City Center
  • Burj Khalifa Shopping
  • Palm Jumeirah

  • The world-famous man-made island shaped like a palm tree. Features luxury resorts like Atlantis and pristine beaches.

  • Best: All Year Palm Island
  • Atlantis Beaches
  • Desert Safari

  • Experience the golden dunes with thrilling dune bashing, camel rides, and traditional Bedouin camp dinners under the stars.

  • Best: Oct-Apr Outer Dubai
  • Adventure Culture
Land of Smiles

International: Thailand

  • Bangkok

  • A vibrant capital known for ornate shrines, floating markets, and energetic street life. A blend of tradition and modernity.

  • Best: Nov-Mar Capital City
  • Temples Nightlife
  • Phuket

  • Thailand's largest island, famous for its clear waters, limestone cliffs, and luxury seaside resorts.

  • Best: Nov-Apr Island
  • Beaches Island Hopping
  • Pattaya

  • A coastal city on the Gulf of Thailand, known for its beaches, cabaret shows, and 24-hour excitement.

  • Best: Nov-Apr Coastal City
  • Beaches Entertainment
Island of Gods

International: Bali

  • Ubud

  • The cultural heart of Bali. Surrounded by rainforests and terraced rice paddies, dotted with Hindu temples and shrines.

  • Best: Apr-Oct Central Bali
  • Culture Nature
  • Kuta & Seminyak

  • Famous for surfing, stunning sunsets, and high-end dining. The center of action and beach life in Bali.

  • Best: Apr-Oct South Bali
  • Surf Nightlife
  • Nusa Penida

  • A rugged island slightly off Bali, known for Kelingking Beach, dinosaur-shaped cliffs, and crystal clear diving spots.

  • Best: May-Sep Offshore Island
  • Adventure Views
