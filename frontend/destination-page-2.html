Explore Kashmir's Most Beautiful Destinations

  • Anantnag: City of Springs

  • Sacred springs and ancient temples make this a spiritual destination. Natural water sources with healing properties attract pilgrims and nature enthusiasts.

  • Best: Apr-Oct 53km from Srinagar
  • Sacred Springs Temples Heritage
  • Kokernag: Garden of Springs

  • Beautiful botanical gardens with natural springs and trout fish hatchery. Landscaped terraces with flowing water create a serene atmosphere.

  • Best: May-Oct 70km from Srinagar
  • Botanical Garden Springs Trout Hatchery
  • Verinag: Mughal Spring

  • Source of River Jhelum with octagonal stone basin built by Emperor Jahangir. Historic Mughal gardens showcase architectural brilliance.

  • Best: Apr-Oct 78km from Srinagar
  • Mughal Heritage Spring Source History
  • Achabal: Royal Gardens

  • Royal Mughal gardens with terraced lawns and cascading fountains. Natural springs feed the elaborate water channels in this architectural marvel.

  • Best: May-Sep 58km from Srinagar
  • Mughal Gardens Fountains Architecture
  • Sinthan Top: Mountain Pass

  • High-altitude mountain pass connecting Anantnag to Kishtwar. Snow-covered peaks and panoramic valley views make it an adventure seeker's dream.

  • Best: Jun-Sep Altitude: 3,748m
  • Mountain Pass Scenic Views Adventure
  • Gurez Valley: Remote Paradise

  • Remote valley near Line of Control with untouched natural beauty. Experience unique Dard-Shina tribal culture and pristine Kishanganga River views.

  • Best: Jun-Sep 123km from Srinagar
  • Remote Valley Tribal Culture Offbeat
