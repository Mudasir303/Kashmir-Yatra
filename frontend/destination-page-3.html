Explore Kashmir's Most Beautiful Destinations

  • Bangus Valley: Alpine Meadows

  • Pristine alpine meadows with diverse wildlife and untouched landscapes. A hidden gem for nature photographers and adventure seekers.

  • Best: Jun-Sep 108km from Srinagar
  • Alpine Meadows Wildlife Photography
  • Lolab Valley: Apple Orchards

  • Scenic valley famous for apple orchards and agricultural beauty. Lush green landscapes and fruit cultivation create a pastoral paradise.

  • Best: Apr-Oct 114km from Srinagar
  • Apple Orchards Agriculture Scenic Valley
  • Betaab Valley: Bollywood Fame

  • Named after Bollywood movie 'Betaab' filmed here. Lush meadows, crystal streams, and snow-capped peaks create cinematic beauty.

  • Best: Apr-Oct 15km from Pahalgam
  • Bollywood Location Scenic Meadows Photography
  • Aru Valley: Trekker's Paradise

  • Base camp for treks to Kolahoi Glacier and Tarsar Lake. Pristine meadows and adventure activities make it perfect for camping enthusiasts.

  • Best: May-Sep 12km from Pahalgam
  • Trekking Base Camping Adventure
  • Tarsar Marsar: Twin Lakes

  • Twin high-altitude alpine lakes surrounded by mountains. Multi-day trekking destination offering pristine waters and breathtaking Himalayan scenery.

  • Best: Jun-Sep Altitude: 3,800m
  • Alpine Lakes Trekking High Altitude
  • Kishtwar: Sapphire Land

  • Famous for sapphire mining and rugged mountain terrain. Remote location offers challenging treks and authentic adventure tourism experiences.

  • Best: Jun-Sep 228km from Srinagar
  • Sapphire Mining Adventure Remote
